The Comedy is a song in which Dante, author and protagonist of the book, finds himself, in the middle of his life, suddenly and without knowing why, in a dark forest. Lost appears to him that the teacher had instructed him to poetry, Virgil, called by Beatrice, beloved by the poet in his youth and then died young, latest in a chain of women running from Maria. Virgil is offered as a guide to lead Dante on a journey of atonement through the underworld by visiting the dead souls, alerting the pilgrim that he can lead him to Hell and Purgatory, but not in Heaven for his lack of faith, to which Dante will be led by soul more worthy of him.
Dante and Virgil meet the sinners of Hell, in which sins are eternal, going down in an inverted cone divided into nine circles according to the vice and sin until the absolute evil Lucifer, to exit in Purgatory represented as a mountain. Here they meet new sinners, in which sins less serious than those of the sinners of Hell are purified with taking consciousness of evil committed, repentance and serve the sentence and the subsequent ascent of the mountain, as Dante himself who is lightened from his sins along the nine rounds. On top of Purgatory, Virgil is replaced by Beatrice, forcing Dante to repentance, a necessary condition to take him to heaven.
The poet through the cornices of Paradise, free from sin comes in the light of Christ incarnate, mystery of humanity, and here the force of Dante, his high fantasy, runs out in his vision. The last verse of the Comedy, the love that moves the sun and the other stars return to the first, in the middle of the journey of our life, in the circularity of the poem, the melody of a song without end.

The Comedy is a poem divided into three parts, Inferno, Purgatory and Paradise, composed in cantos (songs), thirty four to Hell of which the first considered prologue, thirty three to Purgatory and Paradise, for a total of one hundred, the songs are in triplets hendecasyllables.
In Convivio explained how the sense are to be found in a poetic text, identifyng them up to a number of four, literal, allegorical, moral, anagogical.
Strong is the allegory throughout the poem, the dark forest represents the sin, Dante is the ego, the every one representing humanity.

Noble and popular, the Comedy, the work of a Christian influence poetry, art going in life for over seven hundred years with brilliant inventions, music, words, sense apparent and deep, moral and religious as knowledge of good and evil, and redemption of man.
The journey, being at the same time the book author and protagonist of the journey, the relationship between the pupil and the teacher, that one between the poet and the new guide, the meeting and dialogue with characters whose voice is always that of the narrator, the pauses, allow the poet to open constantly new situations, corners, gradients, outlining his vision of the world that by the grace the poet can reveal.
On the one hand the passions of the poet, the civil love for his city Florence and his nation Italy, for politics, the love for a woman, the other his condition of exile from Florence and forced to wander to earn his bread, are driving in his reflection. The protagonist is always the man with his own actions and choices, claim or submission of which the poet is faithful narrator whose natural empathy for humans is often overwhelmed and overturned. Continuous is the comparison with his present world, corrupt and degraded by the Church and by a state unable to govern. The synthesis capacity of the poet defines the characters of the Commedia often in a few verses, short lines, while others require a more complex story, inside the geometric universe of Comedy of which are part of it and build it, as every theme that emerges so well distinguished in the thread that ties all songs themselves and to the work overall, allowing a reading independent for each canto.
Dante does not invent a universe, illustrates the existing one, which has been, is and will be, governed by God, with the natural laws, his word in the Bible, his infinite love, of which man is always in the center, similar to God, able to receive and return his love. The verses of the poem are a real praise to God, the figure of Christ that appears and disappears, is the presence in the background of every page of Comedy, light in which run out the journey granted to Dante by grace that lack his strength, the high fantasy, Christ extreme mystery of inexplicable love also for the poet.
